Funny story...

The founder of Peachy is actually a guy. Three friends went for a beer, after a couple, one of them asked the other two: Girls only use one or two tampons a month max, right? Of course, they cracked up. Suddenly the beers became cosmos and a few hours of “tampon talk” later, he knew a lot more about tampons than your average joe. So yeah, after a few weeks Peachy was founded with the mission of making women lives simpler, more convenient, and happier. Who would have thought a guy could understand it all!
